Section 01mdhub Technical Guide

Native ICD-10 diagnoses and RxNorm medications driven by point-of-care fields.

Conditions carry code, definition, and status; medications carry dosage, indication, and status.

Medications map to RxNorm ingredients including sertraline (SSRI), bupropion (NDRI), and amphetamine and dextroamphetamine (CNS stimulant), alongside atypical antipsychotics, anxiolytics, and sleep agents. A tail of comorbid conditions includes I10 hypertension and J45.909 asthma.

Aligned diagnostic and medication codes give a supervised basis for behavioral-health coding.

PART V · Relational Database Model
Section 05mdhub Technical Guide

A patient hub parenting encounter, with four encounter stores and non-encounter logs.

A parent PATIENT record branches one-to-many into a CLINICAL_ENCOUNTER table with four child stores for transcripts, notes, care plans, and audio, alongside non-encounter logs for messaging, media, and payments, with referential integrity throughout.
