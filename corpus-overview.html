PART I · Operating Scope
Section 01mdhub Behavioral Health Corpus

A purpose-built mental-health record system spanning all fifty United States.

Documentation, telehealth, scheduling, and billing unified for one clinical specialty.

50States covered

mdhub, Inc. is a United States company operating a purpose-built electronic health record and practice-management platform for mental-health workflows, managing clinical documentation, telehealth delivery, scheduling, customer-relationship logs, and revenue-cycle billing. The dataset originates entirely from private clinics located across all fifty states, with defined operational concentrations inside regional provider networks in Florida, California, and the Kansas City metropolitan area.

The database is populated by a provider-reported network of over 10,000 clinicians practicing across approximately 4,500 distinct behavioral-health facilities. Individual regional subscriber networks contain over 600 clinicians who execute more than 50,000 distinct clinical sessions per month.

The mix encompasses the full case-mix of United States mental-health practice: psychiatrists holding MD or DO degrees, psychiatric nurse practitioners (PMHNP, NP), licensed clinical therapists (LCSW, LPC, MFT, and clinical psychologists), and specialized eating-disorder treatment program directors.

A foundation for clinical model development grounded in real United States outpatient mental-health care, recorded at the point of contact.

Conditions & risk coding

Longitudinal text tracks define clinical reasoning for generalized anxiety, recurrent and single-episode major depression, ADHD (inattentive, combined, unspecified), PTSD, OCD, panic disorder, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia, with specialized cohorts tracing restricting and binge-eating or purging variants of anorexia nervosa. Diagnostic fields explicitly index recurrent suicidal ideation, linked to specialized clinical safety documentation, and capture comorbid hypertension, epilepsy, obstructive sleep apnea, and asthma.

Psychopharmacology

Medication records capture active drug names, dosages, indications, and active or discontinued status flags across SSRIs, CNS stimulants and non-stimulants for ADHD, atypical antipsychotics, anxiolytics, sleep aids, and tricyclic antidepressants.

Real-world prevalence and prescribing patterns suited to safety-critical and symptom-severity research.

PART VI · Research Capabilities
Section 06mdhub Behavioral Health Corpus

Clinical concept coverage and research capabilities.

Conversation-to-note generation

Pairing 1,850-word transcripts with signed 840-word notes across roughly three million sessions provides an input-and-output framework to automate chief complaints, histories, and plan assessments.

Safety-critical evaluation

Granular risk factors, protective factors, and suicide-risk determinations across millions of sessions support training and algorithmic evaluation of high-stakes clinical safety logic.

Information structuring & speech

Sub-structured Mental Status Exam notes let models extract discrete psychiatric observations from conversational text, while 1.67 million hours of 16 kHz audio paired with speaker-labeled text support medical speech-to-text and diarization.

Clinician-to-patient translation

Aligned plan sections paired with plain-language after-visit summaries support translating complex terminology into readable, second-person care directives.

A multimodal foundation for documentation, safety, speech, and patient-communication research.
